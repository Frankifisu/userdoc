
Pressure
********

.. _keyscheme PRESSURE: 

::

   PRESSURE pressure {pressure_high npress}

``pressure``
   Pressure (bar) at which pressure the COSMO-RS calculation should take place. Default 1.01325 bar (1 atm). The first pressure in case of a range of pressures. 

``pressure_high``
   The last pressure (bar) in case of a range of pressures. Only used in case of solvent boiling point calculations. 

``npress``
   The number of pressures in case of a range of pressures.
